iPhone is a line of smartphones deaigned and marketed by Apple Inc. It was first introduced in 2007, revolutionizing the mobile industry with its innovative and its user-friendly interface. Since its inception, the iphone has become one of the most popular and iconic smartphones worldwide, setting new standards for design, functionality and performance

Samsung

Samsung is a multinational conglomerate headquartered based in South Korea, known for its diverse range of products and service. Founded in 1938, Samsung has grown into one of the world's leading companies, with a strong presence in various industries, including eletronics, mobile devices, appliances and more.

Redmi

Redmi is a smartphone brand owned by Xiaomi Corporation, a Chinese eletronics company.
